You-Coach

You-Coach is an academic coaching program between students in secondary schools, supervised by the schools.

Students with difficulties in a subject and who want to make progress can ask for explanations and advice to other students in their school who have mastered the subject or have already acquired a good working method.

With You-Coach everything is quick and easy: just register as a Coach or request a coaching session on this website, and it will organize a private coaching session in the school.

Become A Coach

To help others and increase your potential

If you feel strong in a subject and want to help other students, sign up as a coach at your school. You will strengthen your knowledge in this subject, you will develop new skills and above all you will feel useful. Your commitment will be recognized and rewarded by your school.

Benefit from coaching

To improve you academic results and regain motivation

If you have difficulties in a subject and you want to progress, make an appointment for private coaching, and benefit from the academic and practical experience of another student at your school, who understands your concrete needs. Your willingness to progress will be recognized by your school.
